About me

I’m a UX/UI Designer interested in crafting meaningful user experiences that both facilitate and stimulate. My background as a professional pianist and visual artist has given me a knack for solving problems creatively, collaborating with colleagues and clients empathetically, and breaking down seemingly insurmountable challenges into smaller, actionable steps. Furthermore, previous roles as a nonprofit grant writer and communications assistant have honed my ability to write strategic copy, mapping out both ideological and practical components of grant proposals and marketing campaigns alike. To me, the field of UX Design is a perfect amalgamation of my eclectic array of interests, where I can merge research, psychology, and aesthetics to solve real problems for real people. In particular, I am interested in the possibilities for multisensory, audiovisual customer experiences that combine innovation with emphasis on issues of accessibility.
