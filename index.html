Recent work

Muser

I set out to fill a gap in the market for an app where both professional and amateur musicians could get their questions answered by industry experts, while finding new creative communities in the process. For this project, I drew up a problem statement, visualized user journeys and flows distilled from surveys and interviews, and iterated on my hypotheses via wireframes, user testing, and a high-fidelity interactive prototype.
